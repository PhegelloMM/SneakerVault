💭 Why I Built SneakerVault

I've always been a huge sneakerhead — I love the stories behind every pair and the confidence they bring when you wear them. Over time, my collection started growing, and I wanted a fun, digital way to track my sneakers — both the ones I already own and the ones I dream of adding to my rack 👟✨

That's how SneakerVault was born. It's more than just an app; it's a space where I can visualize my sneaker journey, stay organized, and celebrate my passion for sneakers while learning and improving my coding skills 💜
